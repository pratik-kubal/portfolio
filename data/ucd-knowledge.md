## 1. Organization Overview

**Full Name:** University City District (UCD)

**Founded:** 1997

**Mission:** UCD is a partnership of world-renowned anchor institutions, small businesses, and residents that creates opportunities and improves economic vitality and quality of life in the University City section of West Philadelphia.

**Tagline:** "Building a thriving University City for everyone."

**Scope of Work:** UCD works within a place-based, data-driven framework to:
- Invest in world-class public spaces
- Address crime and public safety
- Support small businesses along commercial corridors
- Connect Philadelphians to careers with employer partners
- Promote job growth and innovation
- Host signature community events
- Address homelessness through compassionate outreach
- Operate and advocate for transportation improvements

**Summary Statement:** Every day, UCD works to change places and change lives.

**Funding:** UCD's work is generously supported by contributions from its Board of Directors, UCD Champions, grants, and donations from neighbors.

**Leadership Transition (as of early 2026):** Matt Bergheiser, who served as President since 2009, is stepping down. Sarah Steltz has been appointed Interim President by the UCD Board.

## 4. What UCD Does — Core Programs

### 4.1 Transform Public Spaces
UCD is dedicated to creating and maintaining public spaces that strengthen and enrich University City. Key investments include civic infrastructure and public spaces. Notable public spaces include:
- **The Porch at 30th Street Station** — a popular outdoor gathering spot near 30th Street Station where UCD has hosted food trucks and events
- **Trolley Portal Gardens** — located at 40th & Baltimore Avenue; a site for free family-friendly events

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/transform-public-spaces/

### 4.2 Maintain Clean and Safe Streets
UCD creates a safe and welcoming environment for residents, employees, students, and visitors through:
- **Public Safety Ambassador Program** — managed by Allied Universal in partnership with UCD; ambassadors patrol the neighborhood on bikes and on foot
- **Public Space Maintenance Team** — cleans and enhances more than **160 University City commercial and residential blocks**
- Liaison with law enforcement agencies citywide
- Coordination of major events' safety

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/maintain-clean-safe-streets/

### 4.3 Assist the Unhoused
UCD addresses homelessness through a compassionate, people-first approach by:
- Partnering with **Project Home** and the **Office of Homeless Services**
- Providing individualized, research-driven outreach and case management
- Building personal relationships and providing innovative solutions

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/assist-the-unhoused/

### 4.4 Improve Transportation
UCD addresses bike & pedestrian safety, traffic congestion, and public transit routing. Key initiatives include:
- Operating the **LUCY Bus** in partnership with SEPTA (see Section 7)
- Supporting a **Transit Management Association (TMA)**
- Research and planning for bike/pedestrian safety
- Coordinating with SEPTA and the City of Philadelphia

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/transportation/

### 4.5 Support Our Local Economy
UCD supports the local business community and residents through services including:
- **Small Business Services team** — supports business recruitment, retention, and growth along commercial corridors
- **Project Rehab** — renovated 120 formerly abandoned housing units, unlocking $21 million of real estate market value
- Business assistance and support programs

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/economic-development/

### 4.6 Provide Landscape Services (Green City Works)
UCD operates **Green City Works**, a profitable, full-service landscaping social venture that:
- Employs residents with barriers to employers
- Maintains more than **1.5 million square feet** of green space
- Provides design-build-maintenance landscape services
- Serves clients including Urban Outfitters and others across the region

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/gcw/

### 4.7 Connect Residents to Careers (The Skills Initiative)
UCD runs **The West Philadelphia Skills Initiative**, a nationally acclaimed workforce intermediary (see Section 6 for full details).

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/skills/

## 6. The Skills Initiative

**Full Name:** The West Philadelphia Skills Initiative (WPSI) / The Skills Initiative

**Website:** https://philadelphiaskills.org/

**UCD Page:** https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/skills/

**Founded:** 2011

**Mission:** A bold solution to close the opportunity gap in Philadelphia by building customized talent solutions that bridge the divide between Philadelphians seeking opportunity and employers seeking talent.

**How It Works:** The Skills Initiative runs year-round, cohort-based, jobs-driven training models that harness the hiring power of major area employers while cultivating neighbors' talent.

## 7. LUCY Bus Service

**Full Name:** Loop through University City (LUCY)

**Operated by:** University City District in partnership with SEPTA

**Purpose:** Serves hundreds of thousands of passengers annually between 30th Street Station and nearby jobs in University City.

**Frequency:** Every 30 minutes or more, Monday through Friday

**Hours:** 6:00 AM – 9:00 PM (Monday–Friday)

**Closed on:** New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day

**Routes:** Green Loop and Gold Loop

**New Stop (January 2026):** UCD and SEPTA added a new stop for both the Green and Gold loops at the corner of **37th and Cuthbert Streets**.

**Construction Notice (2026):** Due to construction around Penn Presbyterian Medical Center (ongoing through 2026), SEPTA's online route map may not be current. The schedule is accurate. Download schedule: https://s3.amazonaws.com/schedules.septa.org/current/LUCYGO.pdf

**Free riders (no fare required):**
- Students of the University of Pennsylvania
- Employees of: University of Pennsylvania, University of Pennsylvania Health System, University City Science Center, Children's Hospital of Philadelphia

**All others:** Use the same SEPTA payment methods as the rest of SEPTA's network.

**Schedules:**
- Green Loop: https://www.septa.org/schedules/LUCYGR
- Gold Loop: https://www.septa.org/schedules/LUCYGO

**URL:** https://www.universitycity.org/lucy/

## 16. Chatbot Q&A Guidelines

The following guidance helps the chatbot answer common question types accurately and helpfully.

### General Behavior
- Always refer to the organization as "University City District" or "UCD." Do not confuse it with the University of Pennsylvania or Drexel University (those are anchor institutions, not UCD itself).
- UCD is a nonprofit community development organization, not a university.
- When users ask about events, direct them to the Community Calendar at https://www.universitycity.org/calendar/ for the most up-to-date information, since event dates change seasonally.
- When users ask about job opportunities, direct them to https://www.universitycity.org/careers/ for UCD employment, and https://philadelphiaskills.org/ for Skills Initiative training programs.

### Common Question Routing

| Question Type | Best Resource |
|---|---|
| "What events are happening?" | Community Calendar → https://www.universitycity.org/calendar/ |
| "How do I ride the LUCY bus?" | LUCY Bus page → https://www.universitycity.org/lucy/ |
| "How do I apply to the Skills Initiative?" | philadelphiaskills.org |
| "How do I get help for my business?" | https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/economic-development/support/ |
| "Who runs UCD?" | Interim President: Sarah Steltz; see About UCD |
| "How do I donate?" | https://ucd.networkforgood.com/ |
| "How do I become a sponsor?" | https://www.universitycity.org/doing-business/become-a-sponsor/ |
| "Where is UCD located?" | 3940 Chestnut Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104 |
| "How do I contact UCD?" | 215.243.0555 or ucd@universitycity.org |
| "What is the Dollar Stroll?" | https://www.universitycity.org/stroll/ |
| "What neighborhoods are in University City?" | Includes areas such as Powelton Village, Cedar Park, Garden Court, Walnut Hill |
| "What is Green City Works?" | UCD's landscaping social venture → https://www.universitycity.org/what-we-do/gcw/ |
| "Is the LUCY bus free?" | Free for Penn students and employees of UPenn, UPHS, UCSC, and CHOP. Others use SEPTA fare. |
| "Where are Movies in Clark Park?" | Clark Park, West Philadelphia; every August |
| "What is the Pop-Ups at the Portal event?" | Free performances at Trolley Portal Gardens (40th & Baltimore Ave) |

### Things the Chatbot Should NOT Do
- Do not provide specific event dates beyond what is documented here without directing users to the live calendar.
- Do not make up staff contact information. Only use extension numbers listed in this document.
- Do not claim UCD is a university, school, or government agency.
- Do not provide legal, financial, or medical advice.
- For time-sensitive questions (e.g., current LUCY bus delays, today's event schedule), always direct users to the relevant live page or encourage them to call 215.243.0555.
