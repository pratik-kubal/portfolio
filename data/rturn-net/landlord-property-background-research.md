# Landlord-Property Background Research
Aug 6, 2017 | [News](https://rturn.net/category/news/)
Most tenants have paid a landlord to complete a background check before being allowed to rent. We are now offering you the ability to get objective data on your landlord and/or management company.
We will rate them for free and for those seeking a detailed report, we charge a small fee. This service provides you with material and some analysis to assist you in making informed decisions regarding your landlord-tenant relationship.
You can expect to find out about licensing; liens, eviction record and more. Please click on the following link to give us what we need to serve you:  _**[Landlord Background Research For Tenants](https://rturn.net/background-check/)**_.
