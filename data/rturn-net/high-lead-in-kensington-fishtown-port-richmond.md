# High Lead In Kensington, Fishtown & Port Richmond
Jun 21, 2017 | [News](https://rturn.net/category/news/)
The Philadelphia Inquirer ran a story on dangerous lead levels in the Kensington, Port Richmond and Fishtown neighborhoods. Please  _**[read the article!](https://www.philly.com/philly/news/special_packages/toxic-city/philadelphia-lead-soil-fishtown-construction-dust.html/) **_We thought it was important to inform you about this issue and the preventative methods necessary.
Philadelphia has a problem greater than other cities. The issue is worsened by the dust being blown into the air during construction projects. Now tenants not only have to worry about lead paint in their homes but simply in the air as they enjoy their communities.
