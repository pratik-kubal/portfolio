# Closed Wednesday due to Winter Storm
Jan 5, 2017 | [News](https://rturn.net/category/news/)
TURN is always closed when the Philadelphia Public Schools are closed due to inclement weather. TURN will be closed on Wednesday, March 21th because of the expected winter storm. TURN will reopen during normal business hours on Thursday.
STAY SAFE/STAY HOME
