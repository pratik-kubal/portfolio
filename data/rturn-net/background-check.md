## Landlord/Property Background Check
Most tenants have paid for their landlord to complete a background check on him or her before being allowed to rent. We are now offering you the ability to obtain objective information on your landlord and/or property for $10.
We encourage you to use our services before signing a lease or even paying an application fee; although it still may be useful after you have signed a lease. 
Our goal is to help you avoid bad landlords and/or management companies that will take your rental payments but often fail to hold up their end of a leasing agreement. This service is to help you make an informed decision about your housing.
Our report will include information like:
Z
Owner’s name and mailing address
Z
License & Inspection History
Z
Landlord-Tenant Eviction Record
Z
Liens, Foreclosures, etc.
Z
Lead Inspections
Z
And other relevant information
We will provide you with a “Riskiness” rating, based on the information available, in relation to law and standard practices. Fill out whatever you can below and we will help do our part in your desire for quality housing.
