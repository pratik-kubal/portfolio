# Phase 4 Rental Assistance
Apr 20, 2021 | [News](https://rturn.net/category/news/), [Webinars](https://rturn.net/category/webinars/)
Through the PHLRentAssist program, the City of Philadelphia offers emergency rental assistance to help tenants and landlords who have been impacted by COVID-19. To apply, visit: [phlrentassist.org](https://phlrentassist.org/)
