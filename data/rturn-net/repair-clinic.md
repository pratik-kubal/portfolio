## Repair Clinic
The Repair Clinic aims to educate tenants about their rights when dealing with repair issues. The clinic goes over the tenants’ rights around repairs and follow up steps if needed.
**The Repair Clinic Webinar is conducted every Thursday at 6pm**
To register, please click on the link below. If someone does not have internet access and wants to register by phone, ask them to call **[(267) 635-4130](tel:2676354130)** to process their registration over the phone.
The clinic is for information only and will not provide legal representation or advice.
**It is designed for issues regarding private rental properties in the City of Philadelphia only.**
