# TURN\’S OFFICE IS CLOSED
Mar 1, 2020 | [News](https://rturn.net/category/news/)
Out of concern for the safety of our staff, clients and the entire community during the Covid-19 pandemic, TURN\’s office will be conducting a partial shutdown from until further notice. This closure will not affect the commitments or the essential operations of the organization but there will be no classes or office appointments during this time. Senior management will be available to monitor the situation and address any problems as they arise. This is a temporary arrangement which may change as the situation evolves.
In the event of a housing emergency, the Philly Tenant Hotline will continue to be available at 215-443-2500. Please checkout our Tenant Rights Webinar at www.rturn.net.
[![rturn updated logo](https://rturn.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/rturn-updated-logo.png)](https://rturn.net/)
